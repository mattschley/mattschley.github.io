COVID-19 Spread in America

Cases and Deaths Measured at the Beginning of the Month
July 1st, 2020

This brings us to the beginning of the current month of July. Most of the lockdown efforts have been lifted, with much debate about whether to enter another lockdown or to remain open to help the economy recover.



However, despite the policy disagreements across the country, the data is hard to ignore. Many states are seeing massive spikes in cases and deaths, with most occuring in states like Arizona and Florida where lockdown measures were either looser or where they were lifted earlier. According to the data, not much flattening of the curve has occured, and many states are currently seeing massive spikes that are pushing single day counts that are the highest recorded to date.
